Phishing is a Problem.

Phishing (or scam) emails are sent to billions of email addresses and have the goal of tricking the user into clicking a malicious link to possibly go to a counterfeit copy of a trusted site, or downloading a compromised file or virus. Phishing techniques are improving rapidly, but in my research I noticed several similarities that many share.
The purpose of this site is to educate you on how to spot these similarities and how to avoid falling for a scammer's tricks. By the end of this interactive learning module, you will learn how to detect common inconsistencies and how phishing emails exploit people through basic psychological manipulation.
